## Key Deviations from Paper (Han et al. 2026)

### Now matching
- Delimiter tokens, min_grid ICL masking, vocab +2, rule architecture (3-layer bottleneck), temperature τ=1e-3, gzip filter r>50%, optimizer (Adam, no WD), LR schedule (cosine, 10% warmup), transfer protocol (keep attn+MLP)

### Intentional (speed/scale)
- Model: 350M vs 1.6B
- Rules: 2,000 vs 16,000
- Seq length: 2048 vs 1024

### Known deviations that may matter
1. **Effective batch size:** 64 on 8xH100 (8 × nca_batch=8) vs paper's 16. H200 sweep uses 32, closer match.
2. **Total NCA steps:** ~3,100 (speedrun) vs ~100K (paper). 32x gap, partially offset by our smaller model.
3. **Weight tying:** paper yes, nanochat no (separate wte/lm_head, both reinit'd)
4. **Nanochat scalars** (resid_lambdas, x0_lambdas, smear) reinit'd during transfer — don't exist in paper's model
