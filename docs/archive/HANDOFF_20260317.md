### Key Finding: MLP Transfer Hurts at Higher Rule Counts

At 2K rules, MLP transfer helps slightly (full 0.8553 vs attn-only 0.8564).
At 4K rules, MLP transfer hurts significantly (full 0.8564 vs attn-only 0.8545).

The crossover suggests NCA-trained MLPs become increasingly NCA-specialized with more rules/training, creating harmful initialization for LM training. Attention weights carry the transferable signal (relational/positional structure). This means **all future NCA runs should use `--nca-transfer-mode=attn-only`**.
